## ğŸ“– End-to-End Workflow
1. **Customer** selects products.  
2. **Checkout** scans items, applies discounts, calculates total.  
3. **Payment Processing** handles cash, card, or digital wallets.  
4. **Receipt Generation** provides proof of purchase.  
5. **Inventory Update** reduces stock in real-time.  
6. **CRM Update** logs customer data and loyalty points.  
7. **Finance** records transaction for accounting and compliance.  
8. **Analytics** generates reports for management insights.
