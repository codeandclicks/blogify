## üåê What is E-commerce?
E-commerce (short for electronic commerce) refers to buying and selling goods or services using the internet.  
It involves online transactions where businesses and consumers exchange value digitally instead of through physical stores.

### Why is E-commerce Used?
- **Convenience**: Shop anytime, anywhere without visiting a store.  
- **Global reach**: Businesses can sell to customers worldwide.  
- **Cost efficiency**: Lower overhead compared to physical shops.  
- **Personalization**: Tailored recommendations using customer data.  
- **Scalability**: Easy to expand product lines and markets.

## üè∑Ô∏è Types of E-commerce

| Type | Description | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **B2C (Business-to-Consumer)** | Businesses sell directly to consumers. | Amazon, Zara |
| **B2B (Business-to-Business)** | Companies sell products/services to other businesses. | Alibaba, Salesforce |
| **C2C (Consumer-to-Consumer)** | Consumers sell to other consumers via platforms. | eBay, Facebook Marketplace |
| **C2B (Consumer-to-Business)** | Individuals offer products/services to businesses. | Freelancers on Upwork |
| **D2C (Direct-to-Consumer)** | Brands sell directly to customers, bypassing retailers. | Nike.com |
| **B2G (Business-to-Government)** | Businesses provide goods/services to government entities. | IT vendors supplying government contracts |

## üìñ Commonly Used Terminologies in E-commerce

### üîπ Basic Terms
- **Shopping cart**: Virtual basket holding selected items.  
- **Checkout**: Process of completing a purchase.  
- **Payment gateway**: Service that processes online payments.  
- **SKU (Stock Keeping Unit)**: Unique identifier for each product.  
- **Order fulfillment**: Steps to deliver products to customers.  

### üî∏ Medium-Level Terms
- **Conversion rate**: Percentage of visitors who make a purchase.  
- **Customer acquisition cost (CAC)**: Cost of gaining a new customer.  
- **Average order value (AOV)**: Average spend per transaction.  
- **Omnichannel**: Integrated shopping experience across online and offline channels.  
- **Drop-shipping**: Selling products without holding inventory.  

### üîπ Advanced Terms
- **Customer lifetime value (CLV)**: Total revenue expected from a customer over time.  
- **Personalization engine**: AI-driven system tailoring product recommendations.  
- **Dynamic pricing**: Adjusting prices based on demand, competition, or customer behavior.  
- **Headless commerce**: Decoupling front-end and back-end for flexible experiences.  
- **Cross-border e-commerce**: Selling internationally with localized payment/shipping.
